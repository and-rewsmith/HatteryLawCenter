Lynn Hattery

Lynn Hattery is an experienced litigator whose law practice focuses on meeting the needs and achieving the goals of her clients in the areas of Family Law or Personal Injury. Lynn approaches each legal matter with utmost care and empathy, understanding the effect a case can have on a client and their family. She enjoys helping her clients to navigate a stressful situation and reach financial security and stability. Regardless of the nature of your case, you will appreciate Lynn’s preparation, commitment and results.

Why Hattery Law Firm?

With over 15 years’ experience, Lynn is skilled at identifying the key issues and moving cases to the finish line as quickly as possible. Lynn has effectively represented plaintiffs and defendants in Circuit Court, Juvenile, and Domestic Relations Court. Her clients include several major insurance companies, where she successfully negotiated and tried hundreds of cases. This unique experience provides valuable insights that allow her to negotiate the best offers for her clients and persuade judges or juries as to the value of her client’s case.
